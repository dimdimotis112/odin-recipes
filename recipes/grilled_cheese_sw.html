Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Another tasty homemade meal that everyone can enjoy easily.

Make sure you're hungry because this one is quite the treat.

Ingredients

  • Four slices of toast bread either white or whole grain
  • Cheese that can melt
  • Any sort of meat, be it actual meat or some sausage
  • Greens - tomatoes, pickles

Steps

  1. Take your four pieces of bread and lay them out on the table
  2. Cut up the cheese and put it on each slice of bread
  3. Add your greens like tomatoes or cucumbers on the cheese, whichever you want
  4. Put meat on the bread
  5. Close the sandwiches
  6. Put them in your sandwich cooker and wait for them to be done
  7. Enjoy!
