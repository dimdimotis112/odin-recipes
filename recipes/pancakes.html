Pancakes

Description:

Easy home made pancakes that are just so yummy you don't want to stop eating them.

Follow along this recipe, so you too can enjoy these lovely cakes of the pan.

Ingredients

  • 120g of flour
  • Two tablespoons of sugar
  • One and a half teaspoon of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 250 ml of milk
  • Two tablespoons of melted butter
  • One egg
  • Any toppings you choose or none whatsoever.

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt, sugar and baking powder in a bowl
  2. In a seperate bolw whisk the milk and eggs, add butter once they're mixed thourougly and repeat for a while
  3. Add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture
  4. After the mix is done, heat a frying pan and coat it with vegetable oil or butter
  5. Start adding the pancake mix into the frying pan
  6. Flip to the other side when tiny bubbles start appearing
  7. When all pancakes are cooked you can add whatever toppings you want to the pancakes.
