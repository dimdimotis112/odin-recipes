Noodles

Description

Really fast home cooked meal of instant noodles and a egg.

It tastes surprisingly good and you can add whatever you want to them really.

Ingredients

  • A pack or two of instant noodles
  • An egg or two, can add more
  • Some meat, whichever you prefer and can cook
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Boil the water for the instant noodles and start heating your frying pan
  2. On the frying pan apply some butter or vegetable oil and start cooking both of the eggs and meat if there's any
  3. Once the water is done start preparing the noodles by putting them in a bowl and pouring the water over them, don't forget to add a lid on top of the bowl
  4. Once five minutes have passed, drain the water from noodles
  5. Put some sour cream on the noodles and mix them
  6. Finally add your eggs and meat in the bowl of noodles
